4 - 9 December

Lake Atitlan, Guatemala All Day

Come join us before the wedding. We will be in what many describe as the 'Most Beautiful Lake in The world', Lake Atitlan. The lake is surrounded by three majestic volcanoes that you can hike up to. There are many Mayan communities throughout the lake and the only way to get to each community is a lancha or a boat as there is no road connecting the communities. Each community you will see the beautfiul colors of the Mayan culture as woman wear their traditional attire. You can find a beautiful place to stay on Airbnb, I would personally recommend staying in the villages of Panajachel (the biggest community around the lake), San Marcos de la Laguna, Santa Cruz de la Laguan, and Santa Catarina Polopo as they have the best views of the Volcanoes.
